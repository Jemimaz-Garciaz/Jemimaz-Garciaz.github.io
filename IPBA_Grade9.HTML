YouthTubers

Purpose:

  • We, The YouthTubers will create a YouTube channel that will promote the fundraising event for Sitio Bebe

  • Our group has identified different types of contents that we can choose from to know which content type is suitable for our purpose.

  • Our group has idendified target audience and their needs.

  • Our group aims to provide help and support in solving Sitio Bebe's medical crisis and the fundraising event will gather funds for us to support this project.

  • Here are the final content planning information that our group has decided:


    • Choosen YouTube Content

    Podcast:

  • The audience can listen to what the podcaster has to say. It’s like a radio but pause-able.
  • The audience can listen to different topics, discuss by the podcasters, and get ideas and opinions from them.

    • Review Videos:

  • The audience wants to see how the product works and if they think it's worth it to buy that product we’re reviewing.

    • Target Audience

  • Young adults (15 to 35 years old)

  • Older men and women

    • The Needs of The Audiences

  • To educate them properly about the situation on Sitio Bebe.

  • To advertise the fundraising event

  • To listen to the cry for help of the citizens of Sitio Bebe.
